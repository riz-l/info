About

Myself

I am currently working in website and application development, project management; and, defence and policy recommendation.

Outside of work I'm a big fan of all things tech and all things political. Whether it's a brand new coding framework or the latest political event, my interests are peaked!

I'm always on the look out for new challenges, so if you'd like to work on a project with me, please feel free to get in touch!

International Relations

Web & App Development

Defence & Policy
